Conveyancing


Conveyancing being that one complex area that deals with alienation of interest in land from one person to another by means of appropriate instruments and documents, it involves a lot of drafting of and registry follow ups.

The several steps involved in this process is what we help you navigate until your issue is solved. To help in achieving this we offer the following:

  • Drafting & Registration of Conveyance documents
  • Conducting search on titles and doing due diligence
  • Presenting documents to various registries for booking
  • Navigating of Ardhi Sasa Platform
  • Deed Poll registration
  • Following up on lost tiles, processing of new titles, approval of charges and discharge, registration of caution & Caveat.
