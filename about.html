Learn About Us

Legal service is a complex process that entails a team of skilled professionals offering quality and cost-effective service.
HFarooq Paralegal Services is a firm of young paralegals who have vast knowledge and experience in the legal arena.
Our main goal is working towards delivery of quality, effective and efficient service delivery to all their clients. We are guided by these core principles: Professionalism, Accountability, Diversity and Service delivery.
Our client base includes law firms, companies, commercial banks, financial institutions, insurance companies, investment companies (chamas and saccos), manufacturers, and individuals.

At HFarooq Paralegal Services we pride ourselves with diverse understanding of the need to meet deadlines with satisfactory results, sustained consistency and effectiveness in the provision of quality paralegal services whistle upholding best practice.
